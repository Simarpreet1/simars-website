Pricing Strategies

Strategies

  • Cost Plus pricing
  • Value Based
  • Competition Based
  • Real time pricing

    • Cost Plus Pricing

    Markup pricing is another name for cost-plus pricing. This pricing strategy adds a set percentage to the price of producing a single unit of a good.

    Value Based

    a pricing method that bases prices mostly on how much a customer thinks something is worth. Value pricing is client-focused, which means that businesses set their prices based on what they believe a product is worth to the customer

    Competition Based

    is a pricing strategy in which the price points you set are strongly influenced by those of your rivals. This strategy places the market as its primary focus rather than your costs.

    Real Time Pricing

    often known as dynamic pricing, is a highly adaptable method of determining a product's or service's price. Dynamic pricing aims to enable businesses who offer products or services online to quickly modify prices in response to demand.
